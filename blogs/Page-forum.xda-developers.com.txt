Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro is a 5.9" phone with a 1080 x 2160 pixel display. The Snapdragon 636 chipset is paired with 4/6GB of RAM and 32/64GB of storage. The main camera has a dual lens 12MP+5MP setup and the selfie camera is 20MP. The battery has a 4000mAh capacity.
